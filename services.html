AIRFREIGHT FORWARDING

HOVER SHIPPING specializes in arranging international imports and exports airfreight on behalf of our customers and we have developed fast, competitive and reliable services notably between hubs where we effectively control shipments door to door, thus ensuring both the highest level of security, protecting goods and most immediate transit time available today.

CUSTOMS BROKEARAGE

As a leading Customs Brokerage firm, we have gained unraveled experience in Customs clearance formalities and as such, reduce the unnecessary delays of shipments faced at the port, due to poor documentation, lack of planning and pro-activeness.

OCEAN FREIGHT FORWARDING

HOVER SHIPPING provides full range of ocean freight forwarding services, we cater for FCL (Full container load) and LCL Groupage (Less Container load) and conventional services are booked direct with shipping lines agents that we have chosen for their particular marker specializations and proven areas of service.

Customs handling and freight forwarding services to telecommunications, oil, and the manufacturing sector
