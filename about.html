ABOUT HSC

We give you control of your shipments

Hover Shipping and Commercial Limited was incorporated in 2007 with the Head office at 6, Olakunle Selesi Crescent, Off Asa Afariogun, Osolo Way, Lagos.The company has maintained a leading edge in the provision of customs handling and freight forwarding services to telecommunications, oil, and the manufacturing sector.

Since incorporation, HSC has maintained a principal role in the provision of freight forwarding services to various industrial sectors. This has been achieved by working with qualified and well experienced staff knowledgeable in all aspects of freight forwarding and transport systems all-round the country. In order to fulfil the company’s mission in providing high quality services to worldwide customers and over 50 countries of associate agents, we formed a network of service providers specialized in each individual market with competitive advantage to give our customers the best and the niche services they deserve. It is also our strategy to activate and expand our service network by joining potential forwarders to become a multi-national forwarding consortium.

We are always dedicated to give best service
Why Choose Us

Hover Shipping and Commercial Limited has effectively incorporated multi-modal transport concepts into its day-to-day operations. With this wider vision, cargo no longer travels to gateways ports to await consignee intervention. Our staffs are trained to drive the process, culminating in goods being delivered across multiple borders through various transport means, in the shortest possible time frame.

Another key benefit is having expertise on ground at destination to effectively and efficiently clear your cargo through the potentially complicated maze that is synonymous with customs procedures throughout the developing world. We provide door to door multi-modal services not only to the major trade routes, but also to small, remote and hostile areas, which are not regularly serviced by our competitors. Whatever the project and wherever the destination we will document, plan and execute deliveries without compromising cost competitiveness.

Workforce

Our workforce has operated and successfully completed major projects within and out of the country in association with our international cargo partners. The completion of these projects has called for our staff to be multi-disciplined and conversant with all modes of transportation

Policy

Our policy is to offer our clients high quality, dedicated and personal service, with constant material movement updates, through the use of our regular transportation services and worldwide contacts.
